Mobile Login Page
Use your IdentityIQ user name and password to log into your account. After you log in to IdentityIQ, a quick view of your assigned approvals displays on the Mobile Notifications page. See Mobile Home Page.
For information on recovering your password or unlocking your account, see Password Recovery and Account Unlock Options.
