Section III Using IdentityIQ : IdentityIQ Dashboard : Compliance Dashboard Components
 
Compliance Dashboard Components
• Application Access Review Status — graphic view of the Application Certification completion status for every application in your organization.
• Application Risk Score Chart — graphic view of the risk score for every application to which you have access.
• Application Status — list view of every application in your enterprise to which you have access.
• Access Review Completion Chart — graphical view of the number of certifications that were completed for a given month.
• Access Review Completion Status — list view of certification to which you have access and the completion status of each.
• Certification Decision Chart — graphical view of the certification decisions (Delegations, Mitigations, and Revocations) that were made in certifications that were completed for a given month.
• Access Review Owner Status By Group — graphic view of the completion status of certifications owned by members specific groups or populations. The percentage displayed represents the total number of certifiable items that must be acted on in all of the certifications open against the associated user.
• Access Review Owner Status — graphic view of the completion status of certifications owned by members specific groups or populations.
• Group Access Review Status — graphic view of the certification completion status for every group in your organization.
• Policy Violations Chart — historical look at policy violations over time.
• Risk Score Chart — historical look at identity risk scores over time.
• Signoff Status — list view of the sign off status for tasks and reports on which sign off is required.
