My Access Reviews
The state of your currently active access review requests.
Click the Identity bar to expand a full list of access reviews assigned to you. Clicking on any of the access reviews listed displays the Access Review Details page. See Access Review Details Page Overview.
Click the arrow next to an access review to display the Forward Access Review dialog and forward the access review to a different certifier.
