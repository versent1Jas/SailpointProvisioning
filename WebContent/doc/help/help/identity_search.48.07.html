Risk Attributes
Risk scores and compensating factors are defined when IdentityIQ is configured.
 
Table 5â€”Risk Attributes Panel Descriptions
Criteria
Description
Composite Score
The total composite risk score for the identity.
Role Score
The sum of the compensated risk scores of each role assigned to this identity.
To determine the compensated role risk score, compensating factors are applied to the role base risk score.
Role Score (Base)
The sum of role base risk scores.
This score does not account for the compensating factors defined for role risk scoring.
Entitlement Score
The sum of the compensated risk scores of each entitlement assigned to this identity.
To determine the compensated role risk score, compensating factors are applied to the entitlement base risk score.
Entitlement Score (Base)
The sum of entitlement base risk scores.
This score does not account for the compensating factors defined for entitlement risk scoring.
Policy Score
The sum of compensated risk scores associated with policy violations as defined when IdentityIQ was configured.
Policies do not affect identity risk scores until a violation occurs.
Certification Score
The sum of compensated risk scores associated with certifications.
